Money Buddy

Purpose

        Welcome to Money Buddy, your all encompassing financial assistant.
        Our purpose as a driven trio of High School students from 
        Great Valley High School is to help increase financial literacy amongst 
        our community. Whether it's tracking expenses, estimating future quality 
        of life (QOL) or participating in our extensive courses and quizzes, 
        Money Buddy is here to help. Furthermore, the use of an AI-chatbot 
        in our app helps make answering any questions that you may encounter 
        easier than ever before.
